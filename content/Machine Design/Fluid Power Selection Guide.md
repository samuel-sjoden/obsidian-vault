Fluid power is very useful for large loads because of the large power that can be transmitted with hydraulics.

To select the cylinder and piston refer to the following information
- [[Hydraulic Cylinder Selection]]

There is an adititional challenge in transfering power from a motor into mechanical fluid power. First an approprate motor is required to generate the power. The amount of power required by the system is dependent on the flow rate of the system and the pressure of the fluid, a kinetic (motion) and potential (pressure) energy.

There is a tradeoff between flow rate and speed of actuation and pressure and the amount of force that the system can apply. Force and speed $\to$ power.
