Usually found in low power, low cost applications. They can be very useful in non-critical systems.

## Gear Selection
Start with the material properties of the gear to give a desired allowable stress.

### Stresses In Plastic Gears
The only stresses observed in gear stress that makes a meaningful impact is the bending stress, so it is the primary stress observed.

$$
\sigma_{\text{bending, design}} = \frac{W_{t}P_{d}n_{d}}{FY}
$$
Where the geometry factors $I,J$ have been replaced with the $Y$, the lewis factor.
