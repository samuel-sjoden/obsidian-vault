My understanding of RANSAC leaving the video was that RANSAC is a method of separating data points into inliers and outliers. It does this by sampling random points and fitting a model to them. It then measures how well the model performs (how accurate its predictions of the data). It continues this random sampling a predetermined amount of times based on the predicted amount of outliers in the data. The highest performing model is then used to classify the data points that fit the model as inliers, and the rest as outliers.
